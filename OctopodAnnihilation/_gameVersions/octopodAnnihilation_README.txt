Octopod Annihilation

Controls:
WASD - move character.
Left Click - fire weapon.
Right Click - use active ability.
Scroll - change weapon.

General Information:
All characters start with unique stats, weapon setups, and abilities.
Hover mouse over abilities in character selection screen to get more information.
Only the top row of characters have their abilities implemented currently.
Upon level completion, the level restarts with increased difficulty and new world generation.
